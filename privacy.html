Privacy at StarChart13

Chart generation

Your birth date, time, and location are used in your browser to calculate your chart. This information is not sent anywhere unless you choose to request a personalized reading.

Personalized readings

If you request a $25 personalized reading, the following is sent privately for the purpose of preparing and delivering that reading: your name, email address, birth date, exact birth time, birth location, any optional question you provide, your order number, submission date, and a referral code if you arrived through a promoter's link. This information is delivered privately and is not published anywhere.

We do not collect or store your Cash App password, PIN, banking information, or complete payment credentials. Payments are completed directly through Cash App.

Referral codes

If you arrive through a promoter's link, a referral code is stored in your browser for up to 30 days so that promoter can be credited if you purchase a reading. A referral code never changes your $25 price.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your information with advertisers.
  • We don't store your birth information permanently in your browser.
  • We don't put your personal information in public files, URLs, or analytics.

Questions

Contact us using the email address shown on the order confirmation page.
